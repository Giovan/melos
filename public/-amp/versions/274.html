About this Title:

Some 3,6 million people in South Africa speak Southern Sotho. However, the language is also spoken in Lesotho where different spelling rules apply, i.e. SO (Standard Orthography) and LO (Lesotho Orthography).

Translation of the Bible in Southern Sotho started as far back as 1836 with the complete Bible first being published in 1878.

This text is the newest translation of the Bible and is a thought for thought translation. The translation was completed in 1989. Please visit www.biblesociety.co.za to order a printed copy of this Bible.
