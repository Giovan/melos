Fidela 2013 (RFB13)

Romanian - Română

Published in 2011, 2012 and 2013, the Romanian Fidela Version stands firmly in the tradition of the English King James Version Bible (KJV). The Old Testament is based on the Hebrew Masoretic text and the New Testament on the Scrivener Textus Receptus. The Fidela Romanian Bible was verified by the KJV and care was given to respect the word understandings of the KJV Bible. The stated goal of the Fidela Bible was to have the closest equivalent to the KJV in the Romanian language.
